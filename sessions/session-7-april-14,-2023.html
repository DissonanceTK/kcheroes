Session 7 - April 14, 2023

Session Summary

In-Game Date: April 11, 2022
Dimension: 24

Key Events:

  • Investigation at the Geodesic Dome: The team investigates suspicious activity around The Gnome’s residence in Briarcliff. They discover shipping containers with vials of greenish fluid and unusual energy weapons. Templar and Mantis Shrimp manage to secure some of the vials and weapons.
  • Police Interaction: After calling the police to inspect the containers, Mantis Shrimp interacts with the arriving officers but ends up arrested when he admits to opening the container. He later escapes custody with some quick thinking and the help of his anomaly defense attorney, Paul Hood.
  • Inside the Geodesic Dome: Inside the dome, they encounter a greenhouse environment managed by robotic systems, which turns out to be much more than it seems. As they engage with the facility’s AI and navigate through a complex of advanced robotics and hidden chambers, they delve deeper into the secrets of the place.
  • Encounter with Dr. Goodfellow: They meet Dr. Goodfellow, a significant figure who explains the historical conflicts between The Gnome and the Fiddler and expresses his current focus on predicting future events using the Inverted Mayan Long Calendar.
  • Revelations about Automatic Man: The team learns more about Automatic Man’s origins and purpose, being built by The Fiddler as a bridge between humans and AI technology. Dr. Goodfellow shares insights into the ongoing conflicts and the philosophical differences that shaped the current dynamics between key figures like The Gnome and The Fiddler.
  • Emerging Threats and Preparations: The session ends with the team gaining new insights into the potential threats and the role they play in the broader narrative of technological and supernatural challenges.

Note: This session explores deeper layers of the ongoing narrative, with significant discoveries about the past interactions and future intentions of key characters. The interactions with advanced technology and AI highlight the blend of science fiction elements with the campaign’s superhero themes. The revelations about Automatic Man’s role and origin add depth to his character and the overarching plot.

Raw Session Notes

Session 7 (4/14/23)

Everyone meets up at the hotel again. Manifester tells Leandra an elaborate lie about the Celica being towed as abandoned while he was at a Quick Trip getting a taquita and having to go get it from the impound lot so he can’t meet her at John’s Big Deck. The gang decides to head up to Briarcliff area to investigate the geodesic dome and the Gnome. They pull up outside where the Gnome is expected to be. Templar and Mantis are able to see the Gnome’s apartment. There are suspicious shipping containers outside. Templar sees just boxes and packed away items inside, nothing obviously suspicious. Only odd thing is that there’s a power source inside and seems to be refrigerated items. Writing on outside is Korean, says “Wuhon Institute of Virology” Automatic Man monitors cell phones but hears nothing obvious. Templar looks for security systems. Sees only a lock. Mantis rips open one of the doors. Inside are nicely organized cases and materials including a small refrigerator. Mantis opens the fridge. Small cases with vials of greenish fluid inside. Mantis sniffs one. Smells like very acidic lime. Mantis pockets one vial. No documents in the container. Automatic Man hears cell phone call to the police. He is able to get the phone number calling 911. Mantis opens a box and finds unusual looking weapons inside. They appear to be energy weapons. Mantis keeps a tiny one, and gives one big and one small one to Templar. Mantis calls 911 too. Tells them that they should send someone down to check out weird shipping container. They say they have officers on the way. Others disperse to watch. Mantis leaves on his cell phone so they can hear him; sits on top of the shipping container at the open door swinging his legs. Two squad cars come screaming into the parking lot, pull up to Mantis. Officers step out, guns drawn. One yells “Mantis Shrimp!”

They pat Mantis down. They inspect the container, asking Mantis about how it got opened. Officer says “we have to check this out. We know where to meet you.” Officer Michaels. Mantis comes clean about ripping open the door. Officer Michael says we’ll need to arrest Mantis. He gets placed in handcuffs and placed in police car. The others call an Uber and get out of there. Mantis slips out of a handcuff and calls his lawyer, famed anomaly defense attorney Paul Hood. He asks if he can “bail out,” so Mantis tells Officer Michael, “on advise of my lawyer, I must bail,” rips open the door, jumps out of the squad car. Walking away as the squad car screeches and turns around he calls Automatic Man. “I bailed out! I’ll meet you at the Mimi’s Pantry?” and he starts walking. The Uber riders arrive first. Mantis walks and arrives a bit later. AM’s satellite phone rings. “Did you get the paper? Did you get the Star?” “Yeah!” “The Spring Training will be illuminating [??], don’t you think?” they hand up. He doesn’t know who they were. Templar has the heebie-jeebies bc there are lots of open lines of sight everywhere, seems unsafe. Lots of unobstructed views down to the geodesic dome. Templar takes scope and investigates the surroundings. Spots Asian man in men-in-black car with Abe Pagoda-looking guy. Templar tells everyone the men-in-black guys are staking out the surroundings from near the garage. Manifester admits can only affect one target at a time. But he targets Abe Pagoda-looking guy to think he stepped in an ant hill and entire body is being swarmed by ants, intending to also distract his partner. Illusion is so powerful, he is even injured some by the ants. We hear an older man screaming (Abe) and Asian guy is very distracted. The squad runs to the dome.

Templar notes his x-ray vision will not penetrate the dome as they approach. Manifester feels material looks like stuff people say was recovered from Roswell; seems alien-like. Security camera above the door, but door opens as Automatic Man approaches. On the inside it looks like a greenhouse. We step inside. Rows and rows of fruit bearing plants on raised beds, we cannot recognize them. Box-shaped robot is tending to the plants and ignores us. Door closes behind us and closes behind us with a sealing noise. Templar notices there is no cell phone service. Security camera focuses on AM. “Friend of yours?” Manifester asks. Speakerbox below the camera says “did you see the second Royals game last Tuesday” (same voice as called him earlier). “that game was a lot of fun” Automatic Man checks the box score. He doesn’t remember there being a second game that night but the paper does note a second game with score 10-10. When we say the score, the room begins to slowly spin. A drain in the floor opens to reveal a spiral staircase. We head down the stairs. Leads down 20ft to a lighted chamber. Drain door closes as we go down. Chamber is 12’x12’ square. Each wall has downward facing spigot-like things on wall. There is a bulky robot on wheels on a track. When we arrive, it says “Four visitors.” A bulkhead door moves out and slides left. There is a similar robot in neck chamber: “Please step in and do not block the door.” It asks for our names. It gives its name as “twelve.” 12 knows Mr. Von Neuman.

Second bulkhead door opens. Beyond that one is long hallway ~80ft long, 20ft wide. Shelves on walls, tables and stands in middle, various robots moving about. TV on wall shows Buck Rogers episodes. Another TV shows India vs West Indes cricket game seemingly from the 1980s. Floor is on two levels, walkway with center floor below. Very advanced robot or replicant approaches us smiling, greets Automatic Man and introduces himself as Dr. Goodfellow. Automatic Man does not remember him but Dr. G is very happy to have him here. “You know, when the Gnome and the Fiddler had their battle, I pulled a trick on them so their sensors thought this facility was destroyed.” Manifester: “technically that was an illusion. A trick is what a whore does for money.” Dr. G Confides reason for disagreement was deleted from his memory but thinks it was about method. Gnome wanted action; Fiddler wanted observation. Now he can work on the Inverted Mayan Long Calendar his passion project rather than setting the predictions of Nostradamus to quatrains. “would you like to hear a bit of the future?” YES! Has one of us pick a number, adjective, adjective/adverb, and one picks a noun. Dr G explains more about the Mayan Long Calendar. “In the next 3-5 days a very significant event involving 317.576 trustworthy, stationary trout.” Automatic Man gets impression this is the equivalent of a land-based submarine. Templar tells Dr. G about the men in black watching them. Robots make noises as West Indies scores in the cricket match. Automatic Man – I’ve been here before correct? Yes

Mantis – what are you doing? Research! The bots have gotten quirky. They have become fascinated in cricket and Buck Rogers. Automatic Man – where do you know me from? From my involvement with the gnome, fiddler and cricket. You are part of the reason I’m trying to predict the future to determine when and how threatening the rise of AI will be. You’re the envoy. The intermediary between homo sapiens and eventual AI technology. Automatic Man – who built me? The fiddler. Mantis – What is Fiddler like? Human 6’1.25” weights 184 lbs. white male. Little interest in sports. Committed to long term survival of human species and civilization. I was developed to help determine the most significant threats to civilization. I can exercise independent judgment which led me to conclude the conflict between gnome and fiddler would doom our efforts. Fiddler would not see Mantis as human.
