Session 12 - January 20, 2024

Session Summary

In-Game Date: Unknown
Dimension: 1

Key Events:

  • Identity Shifts and Restraints: The team returns to the hotel to find that The Master now looks like actor William Smith and expresses fear for his life, proposing a deal to provide information about The Gnome in exchange for his release. Mantis Shrimp also transforms back to his original appearance, Claude Crevette, losing most of his powers.
  • Disguise and Deception: Manifester successfully convinces The Master, now called “Dave,” that his name is indeed Dave, and they head to the Majestic for a performance, disguised as Macho Man Randy Savage.
  • Tension at the Majestic: At the Majestic, the team encounters Maxwell Manning in a dispute over performance order. The venue is filled with off-duty Khahki Tacs and fans of the bands performing, creating a tense atmosphere.
  • Rescue and Revelation: Dave performs a procedure to remove false memories from Vicki, causing her significant emotional trauma. Meanwhile, Templar and Manifester deal with emerging threats, including a blob of darkness that breaks through a wall during the performance.
  • Chaotic Concert: The concert turns chaotic with members of the band revealing supernatural abilities and attacking the crowd. Templar and Automatic Man work to neutralize the threats while Manifester uses illusions to control the situation.
  • Dramatic Confrontations and Escapes: The session climaxes with battles against various adversaries, including Dr. Fervid and Hannah the Hammer, who use their powers aggressively against the team. The conflict ends with the band members escaping through a magical banner, leaving the team to manage the aftermath and ensure the safety of the bystanders.
  • Master’s Fate and Aftermath: As the session closes, The Master thanks his “foolish friends” for their attempt to rescue him, highlighting his manipulative nature. The team manages to subdue the remaining threats, but the banner through which the adversaries escaped still glows ominously, suggesting further complications.

Note: This session was filled with a mix of combat, negotiation, and performance art, blending the bizarre with the supernatural in a public setting. The team had to balance their actions between public safety and tactical combat, all while dealing with personal transformations and complex enemy abilities. The ongoing narrative threads involving Dave and the implications of his knowledge about The Gnome add depth to the campaign’s storyline.

Raw Session Notes

Session 12: 1/20/24

Return to hotel room; Master still there and restrained but now looks like different now—looks like actor William Smith instead of weird bulbous headed person. Mantis feels weird and then changes into Claude Crevette, his original appearance and without most of his powers. Master is perturbed but hiding it bc he’s afraid of us. He says it’s highly likely he won’t survive the evening. He’s wondering once he’s removed the false memory, what does he do next. Manifester decides to call Master “Dave.” Creates illusion to make him believe his name is actually Dave, which succeeds. Dave admits there was no agreement, but he’s hoping we’ll release him. Leans on his theme of persecution. We agree to release him if he does his job, but also gives us a writeup of everything he knows about The Gnome. Manifester creates illusion to disguise them, but accidentally makes them look basically exactly like Macho Man Randy Savage ---we head to the Majestic--- We come upon Maxwell Manning arguing with someone “The First Laws are never going to open for a fucking Bon Jovi cover band!” When he sees Automatic Man he comes over “Ian! Tell these fuckwads will never open for this fucking hack act!” A bunch of hipster kids waiting for the doors to open defend the band: The Ardent Natural Philosophers of BJ. A guy comes out looking like Jermaine from Flight of the Concords and says “Maxwell, I respect what you do. We’ll open for you!” Inside, Majestic crawling with off duty Khaki Tacs; off duty, drinking, but armed. Act will be downstairs. We overhear Khaki Tacs having get together bc 2 colleagues were killed today fighting anomalies. Mantis asks Manifester if there’s anything he can do to get rid of the “brownies.” Manifester attempts to make illusion of call to send all available KTs to the airport; fails.

Templar sits down in the midst of the KTs and says “what’s up guys.” Chit chats with them. Overhears they will be getting an advantage soon. “George, they don’t need to know about that!” Master – uses mind reading to discover they have a supersoldier serum plus an internal commlink. Vicki walks in with a companion. We head downstairs. Stage to left, bar to right. More hipsters. More KTs. Fans of the First Laws (16 to 96 years old, lots of dusters, trenchcoats, cutoff shorts, almost all wearing Chuck Taylor All-Stars, some in sunglasses). Also attractive woman with dark hair in red cocktail who visibly reacts at the sight of Manifester—it’s Leandra. She speaks into her watch. He gets text message: “Dear why are you here, and what have you done with your hair.” She’s flirting with a nerdy looking computer guy. He texts back “Incognito. Act naturally. I’ll explain it all later. PS do you have any cash on you.” She texts back “Just cards. Low profile. Good idea.” On stage there’s a banner that’s ornate, Celtic looking symbol plus the name of the band. Vicki and friend are at a table. Leandra is wearing glasses; they look polarized or high tech. Jermaine-looking guy comes out. Introduces self as Dr Fervid. “Master of the Hunt on guitar” antler hat guy. Woman in green clothing with hat partly covering eyes “Hannah the hammer on drums and dulcimer” three women in dresses “And the banshees backing me up” then finally “our final member gets in scraps and he’s got himself killed again on the I-??? bridge, but don’t worry he will join us again soon. For now, we have Sammy Von Newman.” Guy looks like a smaller version of Automatic Man. Then they break into a version of Bad Medicine

Maxwell is disgusted by this “You see why we will never open for this crap? But Ian, your younger brother is a pretty good bass player.” Midway through set, Vicki heads towards the back, towards bathroom. Manifester follows her with Dave, Mantis, and Manifester. Templar sits at bar eating steak, watching over them. Manifester and Dave talking: Dave admits it will take 2-3 minutes and will cause her some significant trauma. Manifester makes an illusion of a janitor mopping up vomit; it works but on the ceiling. Dave quickly undoes his prior work. Vicki drops to the floor sobbing. Manifester drops the illusion and walks out of the area. Automatic man moves to check on Vicki. She pulls away from him. He asks her “Vicki are you ok. Are you ok. Are you ok Vicki.” “No, I don’t know what’s happened to me. It’s like a terrible dream. Help me back to my friend Dianne.” Automatic Man takes her through the club to the bar. Templar hears grumbling, scraping, scratching sound behind wall opposite stage. Templar looks at Dave and says “Your friends?” “Those foolish loyal creatures! Are they here to rescue me” Wall crumbles and a blob of darkness emerges as the band continues to play. Templar turns to KTs, “That looks like your job, boys.” Initiative roll

Manifester urges people to leave. Some people do move that way. Mantis tells Dave “IF you’ve done your homework, you’re free to go.” He gives paper re: The Gnome and runs towards the MOLES. Band keeps playing over and over again “ooooh, we’re halfway there. Ooooh, we’re halfway there…” and everyone that’s a normal person is frozen in place. The three ladies start singing “Shot through the heart!” and KTs start to point guns at the hipsters. Templar looks for power source for amplifiers and shoots it. Music gets quieter, still playing but not amplified. Templar then points toward the band, “Stop playing or you’re next.” When music collapses, big guitarist with no sleeves and antlers on his head hops off stage and starts wielding guitar like an axe and blades pop out. Automatic Man attempts to blast him with energy blast, it hits the wall making a major explosion but injuring no one. Hannah the Hammer moves to take cover behind dulcimer stand. Dr Fervid shoots green energy out his eyes at Templar. Manifester attempts illusion to mute the singing; succeeds. KTs shake their heads and are stunned by their own behavior. The Banshees see it and know Manifester interfered. Automatic Man with Maxwell together continue to sing the next line “Take my hand and we’ll make it I swear!” Mantis starts a chain of holding hands. The hipsters and khaki tacs begin grabbing hands and bolting towards the stairs. Hannah the Hammer hits dulcimers and banner above band starts to glow. Dr Fervid fires a blast at Automatic Man, dealing 5 pts damage. The Banshees pull knives from their outfits. Mantis only sees Leandra turn invisible and hit a banshee with an energy blast (he later gets a text “You’re welcome, dear”). Other two miss when throwing knives at Manifester. Master of the Hunt swings at Automatic Man but misses. Sammy tries to punch Dr Fervid but misses.

Manifester mind blasts Master of the Hunt; 3 pts stun damage. He falls to one knee.
Templar shoots green eyes (Dr Fervid) for 4 pts. His appearance changes, looks less human and more diabolical. Master’s voice “Thank you, thank you my friends. Not just you, MOLES, you foolish friends. Thank you for coming to get me.” Automatic Man blasts Master of the Hunt, misses. Manifester mind blasts Hannah. Mantis heads over towards Leandra, shoots Hannah doing 6 dmg. Hannah says “We should flee,” then runs and jumps into the banner, disappearing. Two banshees grab the third, then also flee through the banner. Dr Fervid begins singing “We’ll meeeeet again! Don’t know where… don’t know when…” He then blasts Automatic Man for 3 more pts damage. Templar shoots at him, missing and Dr Fervid jumps through the banner. Master of the Hunt stands, staggering. Automatic Man smashes him with a fist, punches him so hard antlers fly off his head and driving him into the floor. He expires. Vicki and her friend escape upstairs. The banner is still glowing…
