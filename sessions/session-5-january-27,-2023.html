Session 5 - January 27, 2023

Session Summary

In-Game Date: April 11, 2022
Dimension: 24

Key Events:

  • Combat Dynamics: Automatic Man engages The Master with energy blasts, causing him to recoil and appear shaken. As chaos ensues with gunshots and explosions, Templar, Mantis, and Manifester maneuver around engaging various threats.
  • Barbara’s Escape: Barbara Wells, a key figure in the encounter, warns of The Master’s capabilities and tries to escape the scene. Manifester intervenes to protect her and confronts The Master with an ego attack, causing him significant distress.
  • Explosive Confrontations: A van rigged with explosives is a central threat, with Templar and others attempting to neutralize the danger. Despite their efforts, the van explodes dramatically, creating widespread chaos and damage.
  • Revelations and Accusations: Amidst the tumult, Barbara reveals her backstory, explaining her abduction by the MOLES and her forced engagement with The Master. Her story adds a personal and tragic element to the unfolding events.
  • Automatic Man’s Discovery: Automatic Man learns more about his own manipulation and control, including a disabled internal security system and a device that had been controlling his actions.
  • Dark and Supernatural Forces: The Master, a telepathic villain, continues to manipulate and control, but his powers are challenged by the heroes’ concerted efforts.
  • Endgame at Bossanova Hotel: The group, along with a captured The M.O.L.E.S., heads to the Bossanova Hotel for refuge. There, Automatic Man is recognized and given a key to the penthouse, suggesting a deeper backstory and possibly prior arrangements.

Note: This session is filled with high stakes and rapid developments. The interplay between supernatural abilities, personal backstories, and explosive action creates a complex and thrilling narrative. The session ends with the group seeking refuge and regrouping, setting the stage for further revelations and conflicts.
