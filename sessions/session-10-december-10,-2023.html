Session 10 - December 10, 2023

Session Summary

In-Game Date: Unknown
Dimension: 1

Key Events:

  • Chase and Confrontation: The team intercepts Automatic Man, who has inadvertently become involved in a low-speed chase through the city in Manifester’s Celica. The chase concludes in the West Bottoms, an area marked by desolation and ominous signs.
  • Magnetic Crane Incident: Upon arrival, they find a magnetic crane holding the Celica aloft with Automatic Man inside, seemingly affected by the magnetic field. The crane appears damaged and is emitting smoke.
  • Hostile Environment: The scene is fraught with danger, including a heavyset person in makeshift armor controlling a wind funnel of debris, and several hidden assailants in nearby buildings. Templar and Manifester take tactical positions to handle the threats from different angles.
  • Dynamic Combat: As combat unfolds, Mantis Shrimp tries to communicate with the armored person but ends up getting pulled into the debris funnel. Templar shoots an assailant who then becomes part of the chaotic debris cloud.
  • Rescue Attempts: Voices calling for help are heard from a nearby building, adding a layer of urgency to the already tense situation. Templar moves to investigate and provide aid.
  • Supernatural and Technological Manipulations: The adversaries use a combination of physical attacks and supernatural or technological enhancements to challenge the heroes. Manifester’s attempts at creating illusions to disable the armored person initially fail, increasing the peril for the team.
  • Climactic Moments: The session culminates with Templar being pulled into the cyclone and Mantis Shrimp making a desperate attempt to dislodge the Celica from the magnetic crane. Automatic Man, stunned and disoriented, ends up pressed against the window next to Mantis Shrimp’ head, highlighting the chaotic and unpredictable nature of the battle.

Note: This session is filled with intense action and complex scenarios, testing the team’s abilities to adapt and respond to multifaceted threats in a hostile environment. The presence of civilians in distress adds emotional stakes to the encounter, while the mysterious abilities of the adversaries introduce new challenges that could have significant implications for future sessions.

[Jerry is smart, able to detect that we are from an alternate universe. Jerry cold and calculating; Blurry takes more risks, is enthusiastic, leader-like but not ranked.] Manifester is getting repeated texts asking why his car is on the news. TV turns on low-speed chase following Manifester’s Celica, Automatic Man is driving. [Automatic Man was run through computer simulations to develop his abilities and… he used Grand Theft Auto as his simulator] Manifester can feel a connection with the CD player despite being many miles away. Car is in River Market, headed towards West Bottoms somewhat erratically and badly. We stop at Gates for food before heading out to intercept Automatic Man. We meander towards him. An ARIES starts following us. As we get into West Bottoms, we pass barrels w graffiti of happy face with horns, ARIES vehicles stop following once we get there. We get to about 1104 Union Ave. Area is desolate. A Blackhawk helicopter is crashed on its side, smashed by blunt objects. A large magnetic crane is raised with Manifester’s celica attached to the magnet. Automatic Man is inside, looks exhausted, perhaps due to the magnetic field. Seems stunned. Nobody seems to be operating crane but smoke is coming out of it, not in great shape. Just past crane is a heavyset person wearing makeshift armor. In buildings can hear people scurrying about.

Templar detects movement on fifth floor of building to right, hears sound of automatic weapon cocking. In building on left, third floor, hears clanking of chains as a person has it on them or are chained up. Ahead hear wind swirling near the heavyset person, catching dust, small objects, rebar. An 8-foot horizontal cylinder of air begins to form. Mantis rolls down window and shouts towards heavyset person. Templar hears someone ducking down and cocking gun. He has supercar gun target the source of noise. (Order: Templar, Manifester, villains, Mantis) Templar turns car into defense mode and bales out, heading towards left parking garage, crouches behind a burnt out car. Manifester teleports up into building at fourth floor. Someone begins firing automatic weapon at Templar, missing but hitting the burnt out car. Wind baton is building up with larger and larger objects. Smoke coming out of crane is picking up. Mantis walks into the cylinder and catches a radiator from the debris, begins straightening it. Tries to talk to the heavyset person in armor, but they just flick him off. Templar shoots back at person shooting toward him; shot through the mouth, staggers and falls through the window to the ground. He (and the gun) begins churning in the debris field. Manifester runs to the end of the building. He has a good view of the crane and guy in armor; can tell he’s controlling the debris. Crane motor looks like it’s going to go in 3 turns. Manifester attempts to give stocky armor guy illusion of blindness; fails.

Stocky turns the debris into more of a funnel cloud. Mantis gets pulled up into the cloud. The metal of the pole Mantis uncoiled from radiator sticks to the magnetic dish. [Ion pack: have flight for 1d4 rounds, then takes 1d4 to recover] From building on left, Templar hears woman’s voice saying “help me! Help me!” and masculine voice saying “woman don’t get them involved. They’ll just get killed” Templar begins climbing to move into the building Manifester now attempts to give stocky guy illusion of sensory deprivation. Chunky armor guy sends cyclone towards Templar. Dead body smacks into Templar giving 4 pts damage and he’s pulled into cyclone, spinning through the air. Leaves Mantis hanging from the rod in the air. Mantis climbs up the radiator pole to underneath the car, grabs the frame and yanks in attempt to dislodge car, fails. The car only turns, still attached to magnet, driver side up. Automatic Man falls into the passenger side, his stunned face hits the window right next to Mantis’ head.
