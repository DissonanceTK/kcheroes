Session 3 - December 18, 2022

Session Summary

In-Game Date: April 11, 2022
Dimension: 24

Key Events:

  • Continued Chaos from Previous Session: After Mark Renallo’s brunch with Mantis Shrimp, chaos erupts as Mantis resizes under a BMW, causing it to flip over two individuals handling a weapon transaction. The BMW crashes, and gunshots ensue.
  • Intervention by Trenchcoat: A man in a trenchcoat emerges from a “Handsy Plumber” van and fires an energy beam from his hand, incapacitating a shooter. This person is later engaged with Templar in a confrontation that seems to have no effect on him.
  • Mantis’s Actions: Mantis uses an accordion to knock down a man holding the suspected weapon case, which contains what appears to be plastic explosives. He later removes this bomb and hides it under a car.
  • Manifester’s Teleportation and Illusions: Manifester teleports using a swirl of black and purple mist to assist and create distractions during the fight, including illusions to immobilize Trenchcoat.
  • Supernatural Elements: Voices are heard in the heads of the characters, directing the dark blobs and commenting on the situation, indicating an unseen psychic or supernatural controller. Mantis Shrimp sees invisible beams of energy, suggesting the presence of forces hidden from normal sight.
  • Bloody Retreat and New Attacks: The duffle bag holder is left bleeding severely as the dark blobs retreat into a tunnel. A new character with a submachine gun appears, firing at Trenchcoat without effect.
  • Final Confrontations: Mantis pursues the retreating dark blobs into a tunnel, while Templar and Manifester deal with the aftermath and regroup, contemplating their next moves.

Note: This session deepens the complexity of the conflict with multiple physical and supernatural confrontations, introducing new mysterious characters and unseen forces manipulating events behind the scenes.
