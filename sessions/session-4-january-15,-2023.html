Session 4 - January 15, 2023

Session Summary

In-Game Date: April 11, 2022
Dimension: 24

Key Events:

  • Chase and Combat Continues: In the dark, Mantis unsuccessfully tries to tackle a darkness blob in a tunnel, while others see a mysterious woman named Barbara emerge, accused of betrayal by an eerie voice. Manifester attempts to protect her from the blobs.
  • Automatic Man’s Revelation: Automatic Man discovers he has been manipulated and his systems compromised. He disables a control device on his arm, regains some autonomy, and learns of a van rigged with explosives linked to a proximity trigger.
  • Heroic Efforts: Mantis engages blobs with physical attacks while Templar and Manifester handle different aspects of the chaotic scene. Templar makes strategic moves in a parking garage, while Manifester teleports with Barbara to safety.
  • Explosive Situations: Automatic Man tries but fails to locate the proximity detector. He then confronts ongoing threats, including a new character, Fire Cube, who manifests fiery cubes.
  • Telepathic Manipulations and New Threats: The Master is identified as a powerful telepath controlling others. As the battle escalates, Fire Cube aggressively engages, and Manifester attempts to keep Barbara safe from the unfolding violence.
  • Climactic Actions: Mantis continues his effort to mitigate the bomb threat by relocating the explosive’s contents, unaware of the van’s rigged setup. The session ends with the characters scattered in various actions, facing multiple threats and trying to thwart a potential large-scale explosion.

Note: This session intensifies with numerous twists, including Automatic Man’s realizations and the introduction of new adversaries. The session encapsulates a blend of physical confrontations, strategic retreats, and mystical manipulations, setting the stage for future conflicts.
