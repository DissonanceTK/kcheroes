Session 9 - November 19, 2023

Session Summary

In-Game Date: Unknown
Dimension: 1

Key Events:

  • Combat and Aftermath: The team, including a new ally named Blurry, deals with the fallout from previous encounters with villains like Meat Shield, Mosh Pit, and Molotov. Blurry has personal stakes in these battles, having previously fought alongside Templar and experienced tragic outcomes like individuals falling into comas due to Mosh Pit’s influence.
  • Mission to Save Comatose Victims: After retrieving a blimp and dropping it off, the group, guided by Blurry, retrieves Templar’s upgraded Humvee equipped with high-tech modifications. They then set off on a mission to intercept vehicles suspected of transporting comatose victims, with Blurry and Jerry debating the best approach to take.
  • High-Speed Chase and Tactical Engagements: The mission escalates into a high-speed chase, with the team using illusions, tactical maneuvers, and direct confrontations to handle threats from Khahki Tacs (KTs) in troop-carrier vehicles. Mantis Shrimp, Templar, and Manifester work together to stop the vehicles and engage the adversaries.
  • Rescue and Recovery: After intense confrontations, the team successfully rescues three people locked in gurneys inside one of the trucks. Manifester’s illusions play a crucial role in reviving the comatose victims, leading to their safe extraction.
  • Blurry’s Revelations and Alternate Reality: The mission concludes at a safehouse, where Blurry reveals details about an alternate reality version of Kansas City, characterized by heavy surveillance, the illegality of cash, and FEMA’s significant involvement. This setting raises questions about the dimension or reality the team is currently navigating.
  • Discovery of Dimensional Differences: Jerry recognizes that Templar is not from their dimension, hinting at broader dimensional or reality shifts affecting the team. This discovery aligns with the unusual changes noticed earlier, such as the different president and other anomalies.

Note: This session blends high-octane action with deeper narrative developments, exploring themes of alternate realities and the consequences of supernatural conflicts. The team’s dynamics are tested as they navigate complex challenges in a possibly alternate Kansas City, facing both physical threats and the ethical dilemmas of intervening in dire situations.

Raw Session Notes

Other hero has fought Meat Shield, Mosh Pit, Molotov three times. He once fought them with Templar in a church. This is second time he’s killed Mosh Pit. When he killed MP before, some people who were under his trance went into comas and are still in them. Cell phones go off—“blurry 51-50 H H” “Wow, those people are being moved. Templar, Jerry got your car fixed. Lets get it.” New guy’s name is Blurry. They drop off blimp outside Freddie’s Trout House then head to garage with Blurry’s directions. See car with cover over it. Garage setup. Blurry is excited to show Templar the car. Pulls cover off to reveal super car: stealth’ed out Humvee with retractable turret, James Bond-style goodies. Starts up and computer console screen brings up “Jerry.” Jerry thinks bad idea to try to help the comatose people who are being moved; Blurry and he argue about whether to save them or just monitor the movement. Blurry thinks Manifester can get them out of the coma. Blurry explains comms will fall off as soon as he phases to use his powers. Jerry confirms 3 tactical looking troop-carrier vehicles carrying people; expect 2 are decoys. We leave in Templar’s Humvee. Blurry warns of someone setting up with weapon on an overpass. Templar misses with guns, but Manifester makes illusion that car is slightly off to the side from where it actually is. They shoot at it and Manifester makes it look like they were blown up. Templar catches up to the three trucks, pulls ahead of them. Mantis jumps onto roof of first one. The trucks spread out. Khahki Tacs lean out windows and aim guns at Mantis

Manifester mind blasts one of the shooters: Ripple like a heat wave shoots through the air, shooter collapses dropping gun, skitters down the highway Vehicles bear down on Templar Templar has targeting system aim at drivers and PA system says to “Stop!” Vehicles slam on brakes. Mantis can’t hold on and flies off, tumbling along the ground unceremoniously. Templar brakes and turns car dramatically, then steps out guns drawn. People from the vehicle begin producing weaponry, poking out windows/hatches and some exiting and taking cover behind them. Manifester jumps out of the supercar with swirling mists and grand gesture. Attempts mass illusion for KTs of fireballs raining from sky to make them take cover; fails. With another swirling mist and cloud of smoke, he scurries behind the car. Blurry over comms says “I’m about to go out of focus. I’m starting with the middle one.” KTs begin firing Mantis and vehicle; not even close to hitting. Hit vehicle and no damage done. Templar advances on one of the side vehicles, shoot point blank into engine block and… does nothing. Mantis uppercuts the engine block and demolishes it. Second vehicle goes blurry and all KTs fall out onto the highway. We hear Blurry yell “It’s not number 2” Third vehicle squeals it tires, trying to take off. Templar blasts out it’s tire, slowing it down. They’re still driving on the rims.

Mantis jumps towards fleeing vehicle, but falls just short, falling just in front of the back tire, which rolls over him. KTs open fire on Templar, who rolls underneath the truck for cover then shoots with ricochet to hits the other front tire and two KTs. Mantis attempts to pick up the vehicle; grabs the bumper and it rips right off. Manifester makes illusion that vehicle is on fire. They flee. Templar checks out the back of truck. There are three people locked in gurneys inside with medical monitors, unconscious. Manifester uses mass illusion on the comatose people of quiet. They almost immediately start to stir and open their eyes, completely disoriented and confused. We pack them into Templar’s vehicle and drive off. Jerry is annoyed and surprised that the mission worked. Seems to be family of Blurry. Blurry has a safehouse in autobody place near 19th and Vine. 1580 E 19th Street. Jerry quickly realizes Templar is not their Templar; sees Templar is not from this dimension. Meet ragtag group running a resistance to the government. In this reality, KC basically partitioned off. Cash is illegal; use digital currency only. FEMA is super involved. Loads of surveillance. Manifester’s phone rings – caller ID “The Wife” “I’m not going to pick that up.”
